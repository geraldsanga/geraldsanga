About

Gerald is a Full Stack and SQL Developer with experience in building websites and working with data. His background in working with the web, inform his mindful but competitive approach. Fueled by the passion to always learn something new, Gerard considers himself as a 'forever student' and is eager to both build on his skills by staying in tune with latest technologies.

Gerald believes that technology is the vital reason for the huge and undenialbe change in mankind - a tenet he lives out through his interests in astronomy, mindfullness and meditation. He is currently freelancing and is always interested in a challenge, ready to work in anything that will make a difference in the world
